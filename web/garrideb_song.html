A Garrideb Song

by Robert N. Brodie, BSI

(To be sung to "Harrigan" with apologies to George M. Cohan)

G-A-double R-I
D-E-B
Spells Garrideb (Garrideb)
Proud to be a part of Sherlock Holmes's scene,
Keeping the memory of the Master bright green.

G-A-double R-I
D-E-B
You see,
It's the name and the fame
Of our Scion Society,
Garridebs are we.
